About Us

Maple & Yolk

Founded on a passion for exemplary breakfast and lunch cuisine, Maple & Yolk stands as a testament to quality, freshness, and culinary innovation. Rooted in the belief that the day's early meals are pivotal, we've created a menu that blends classic comfort with contemporary flair.

Our dedication to using locally-sourced, high-quality ingredients ensures every dish is not just a meal, but an experience. The warm, welcoming atmosphere of our restaurant reflects our commitment to community and the joy of shared dining. Maple & Yolk isn't just a place to eat; it's a destination where every visit is an opportunity to savor life's simple pleasures.

At Maple & Yolk, we believe that a great day starts with a great meal. Our passion for fresh, high-quality ingredients and the joy of bringing people together over food is at the heart of everything we do. We're dedicated to creating not just meals, but memorable experiences for every guest who walks through our doors.

Mike Chloros Owner/Operator
